## Project Overview

The **Uber Power BI Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw Uber ride booking data into meaningful visual insights, enabling users to analyze booking trends, revenue, trip distance, vehicle performance, customer behavior, payment methods, and operational KPIs.

The project demonstrates end-to-end dashboard development, including data preparation, DAX calculations, KPI creation, interactive visualizations, and business-driven reporting.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to provide decision-makers with a centralized dashboard for monitoring Uber's business performance. Through interactive reports and KPIs, the dashboard helps identify booking trends, revenue patterns, customer preferences, vehicle utilization, and operational performance to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dataset

The project uses an Uber ride booking dataset containing transactional and operational information, including booking details, ride distance, booking value, vehicle type, payment method, booking status, customer information, pickup and drop-off locations, and booking dates.

---

## Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Power BI was used for dashboard development, data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI creation, and interactive visualization. DAX was used to create custom measures and business metrics for analytical reporting.

## DAX Measures

Custom DAX measures were created to calculate key business metrics including booking count, completed bookings, total revenue, average ride distance, total ride distance, customer count, revenue contribution, lost bookings, and dynamic date analysis.
